Dunlop Cushyfoot Anti-Vibration Mountings

Cushyfoot Mountings

Cushyfoot Mountings

Available in three sizes - 'A', 'B' and 'S' series. 'S' series is available in special construction with built-in buffer. Suitable for diesel gensets, blowers, compressors, machine tools, pumps, engine test beds, food precessing machinery etc. Rubber porion fully covered to avoid contamination. With Bottom Friction Pads totally flexible suspensions are possible. Levelling device can be incorporated additionally.

Double - U Shear

Double - U Shear

Available in three different sizes. Double - U shear mountings can be used with advantage on instruments and electronic apparatus, protecting equipment in packing cases, suspending light components/recorders in a vibrating equipment, electrical control panels, suspending ducts and pipelines. Can be pre-compressed and used as buffers.

Rectangular Sandwich Mountings

Rectangular Sandwich Mountings

Available in three sizes. Can be fitted under direct compression/shear or at different inclinations to suit diverse application requirements. Ideal for suspending small engines for mobile application, hoppers, heaby installation carriage construction etc. Maximum load carryinng capacity 1000 kg in direct compression and 200 kg in direct shear.

Two-Bolt Instrumountings

Two-Bolt Instrumountings

Highly useful in light-fittings, particularly vapour lamps and high-bay fittings, radiator mountings, small electronic recorders. Maximum load carrying capacity 45 kg in compression and 18 kg in shear.

Dunlop Circular Mounts

Dunlop Circular Mounts

Dunlop Circualr Mounts are low height in construction, which is desirable when "Slenderness ratio" is high. Provision can be made to incorporate a built-in levelling device which is bery much necessary for most of the applicaions. Special construction eliminates the necessity of foundation bolt, marking installation easier and less time-consumeng. These mountings are ideally suitable for machine tools, Presses and Equipments with low base area (about 1 sq. mtr.) but height more than 1.5 meters.
